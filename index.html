English Adjective Learning

How to Play

  • You will see one English word on the screen.
  • Tap the word to see its Japanese meaning.
  • Decide if the word is an Adjective or Not an Adjective.
  • Tap the green button for Adjective.
  • Tap the red button for Not Adjective.
  • You will hear a voice saying Correct or Wrong.
  • Your score increases when your answer is correct.

You can also check the Adjective List and Not Adjective List inside the game.
